Effective Field Goal%

Basketball, some might think of it as a game of luck and chance, ofcourse we all know that there is certainly a level of innate ability involve in it however, to the common fan that is the level of understanding that willing are willing to stop at. Those who consider themselves basketball nerd on the other hand have a much deeper drive to understand the game in a more complxed and statistics driven manner. This is where advanced stats come in.


What is Effective field goal %

Effective Field Goal percentage (more commonly referred to as EFG) is a statistic that measures the weight of a players scoring contribution to a game , considering the fact that a 3 point field goal is a half point more influencial than a 2 point field goal. Thus the statistic was born

EFG Perspective

Some might consider the stat unnecessary, so let's look at it deeper. We have our star center George who lets say shot 8/16 from the floor, impressive! George has a field goal% of 50% but he did not take a distance shot so his EFG stays at 50%. Oh look, we have Tom's stats right here our role playing shooting guard who went 5 of 12 from the field Wow, thats lackluster, only 42%? However Tom shot 3 for 4 from the 3 point line, so when adjusted from EFG we see that tom shot a reasonable 54.2%, thats more impactful than George.
