Retrospective

Wardell Stephen Curry II is considered to be the greatest shooter ever to grace the sport of basketball due to his ridiculous to shoot what seems to be difficult 3 point shots at a very high percentage. Curry is the only player to ever be unanimously voted as the league MVP in a season and also holds to current record for most 3 pointers made (and he is still playing).
Curry has manage to become on of the most beloved players in the NBA despite is globally detested pairing with another one of our top 25 players Kevin Durant from 2016 to 2019. A Stint which saw Steph win 2 more rings after initially winning in 2014-15, eventhough steph managed to win a ring before the arrival of Durant a claim was made that neither man was able to win without the other and that their success hinged on the ability of one another. Steph would go on to prove this claim wrong by again winning a championship ring in the 2021-2022 season. Do not believe that Steph was just able to spawn with this otherworldly talent however, it runs in the family, his father being former NBA player Dell Curry and his brother Seth Curry is also an NBA player who is fairly good at the 3 point shot. This is a testiment to Dell's effort to train his sons in the right manner and of course their perseverance through the struggle of not meeting the norm for NBA height and athletism.
